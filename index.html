About

An intimate 3-night party with local and international DJs, craft cocktails and immersive visuals.

Weekend Rhythm brings together a curated selection of DJs and performers across three nights. Expect deep grooves, tight sound, a friendly crowd and a bar stocked with signature cocktails. The venue offers an intimate dancefloor and vivid VJ visuals — perfect for losing time.

  • Immersive lighting and VJs
  • Quality sound system
  • Signature cocktails & safe space
